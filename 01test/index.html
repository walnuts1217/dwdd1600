Digital Citizenship

what is Digital Citizenship?

Digital Citizenship is using the internet responsibly, safely and ethically.

what is Digital Law?

Digital Law is a set of laws the govern over how people use the internet.

what is AUP?

AUP is Acceptable Use Policy. which are rules that tell you how you may use techology within an organization.

what is plagiarism?

plagiarism is using someone elses work without giving proper credit.

it's still plagiarism even if you do it accidently!

what is Academic Dishonesty?

Academic Dihsonesty is when a student misrepresent their actual knowlege on a subject.

what are the rules for AI generated content?

AI content is only acceptable if allowed by the prof or teacher and only when used responsibly.

AI is a tool for learning not a replacement.
